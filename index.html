🗺️ HOW TO PLAY

In PunFiction: 1-Star Travel Reviews, your goal is to guess a hilarious punny location name based on its terrible 1-star reviews!

🦉 THE "GRAND CANYON" EXAMPLE:

Terrible Review: "It's just a big ditch. I could dig this in my backyard if I had a free weekend and a decent shovel."

More Reviews: "Tried to use it to color in my coloring book, but it was too heavy and ruined the paper."

Guess the Parody Location: ???

This puzzle involves matching the clues to the correct location pun name.

Real Location: Grand Canyon

Ah ha! The parody answer is THE GRAND CRAYON (Rhymes with Canyon!)

💡 Pro Tip: Use the progressive hints at the bottom if you get stuck! They show the real location, load extra reviews, and even fill in letters and vowels!
